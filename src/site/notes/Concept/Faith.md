# What is faith?
This page will be updated with more insights as the Lord prompted. 
When reading **Hebrews 11**, understand this:

- Faith **is not** a fuzzy feeling
- Faith **is not** empty words
- Faith **is revealed through action** ([[KJV Tagged + Lexicon/Bible 2/59 - James/James 2#17\|James 2#17]])
- Faith means **placing everything on the line** for what you believe
- Faith is living in such a way that others could say:  
    _“If God is not real, then you are the most to be pitied.”_

Faith, like gold, is only shown to be precious **when it is tested**.
